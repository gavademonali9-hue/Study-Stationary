About Gaudwadi 🏡

“A model for pomegranate excellence in Sangola Taluka.”

Gaudwadi village in Sangola Taluk, Maharashtra, is celebrated for its inspiring pomegranate cultivation. Recognized by the District Collector, it stands as a benchmark for sustainable and profitable farming, thanks to its advanced drip irrigation, community effort, and love for the land.

"गौडवाडीची माती म्हणजे अनमोल ठेवा, तिथं उगमतो डाळिंबाचा राजा – कष्ट, प्रेम आणि परंपरेचं प्रतीक!"

History Of Gaudwadi

Gaudwadi is a historically rooted settlement located within the boundaries of Dalimb village. The majority of its residents traditionally depended on agriculture and animal husbandry for their livelihood.
